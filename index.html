VALORANT

Valorant is a popular free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. The game was officially released on June 2, 2020, after a closed beta period that began in April 20201.In Valorant, players engage in 5v5 matches where they take on the roles of unique agents, each with their own special abilities. The game combines precise gunplay with tactical abilities, requiring players to think strategically and work as a team to outwit their opponents. Matches are played in a series of 13 rounds, with teams alternating between attacking and defending. The game features a variety of modes, including Competitive, Unranked, Deathmatch, and Spike Rush, offering different ways to enjoy the gameplay. Valorant’s maps are designed to encourage creative strategies and spectacular plays, making each match a unique experience

REAL MADRID

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly known as Real Madrid, is one of the most prestigious and successful football clubs in the world. Founded on March 6, 1902, as Madrid Football Club, it was later granted the title “Real” (meaning “Royal” in Spanish) by King Alfonso XIII in 1920.In domestic football, the club have won 71 trophies: a record 36 La Liga titles, 20 Copa del Rey, 13 Supercopa de España, a Copa Eva Duarte, and a Copa de la Liga. In international football, Real Madrid have won a record 34 trophies: a record 15 European Cup/UEFA Champions League titles, a record six UEFA Super Cups, two UEFA Cups, a joint record two Latin Cups, a record one Iberoamerican Cup, and a record eight FIFA Club World championships.
